About SRJahir Stocks

India's market data, simplified.

What is SRJahir Stocks?

stocks.srjahir.in is a free, open-source Indian stock market tracking platform built by SRJahir Tech. Our mission is to provide clean, fast, and easy-to-understand market data for everyday Indian investors.

Whether you are a beginner learning about Nifty 50 or an experienced trader tracking F&O expiry dates, this platform offers tools and data that are genuinely useful without the clutter of traditional financial websites.

What We Offer

Our platform provides live market indices (Nifty 50, Sensex, Bank Nifty and more), the latest market news from trusted sources, an IPO calendar with upcoming and recent listings, top gainers and losers of the day, curated stock picks and analyst recommendations, a SIP and lumpsum calculator, F&O expiry countdown with an economic calendar, sector-wise market analysis, a personal portfolio and watchlist tracker, and educational articles about the Indian stock market.

How It Works

All data on this site comes from publicly available sources. Market indices are powered by Google Finance through Google Sheets. News, IPOs, and movers data is fetched via NSE data sources and stored in Google Sheets. Stock charts are provided by TradingView widgets. Everything runs on a static site hosted on GitHub Pages with no server costs.
