Compounding Ka Jadoo — Rs 500 Monthly Se Crorepati Kaise?

By SRJahir Tech · March 2026 · 7 min read

Albert Einstein ne compounding ko "duniya ka 8th wonder" kaha tha. Aur honestly, agar tum ye concept samajh lo aur jaldi start karo — toh financially free hona possible hai. Compounding woh cheez hai jo Rs 500 per month ko crores mein badal sakti hai. Kaise? Chalein samajhte hain.

Compounding Kya Hai?

Compounding matlab — tumhara paisa kamata hai, phir us kamaye hue paise pe bhi paisa kamta hai, phir us pe bhi... ye chain reaction chalti rehti hai. Example — tumne Rs 10,000 invest kiye 12% return pe. Saal baad Rs 11,200 hue. Ab agla saal 12% Rs 11,200 pe lagega (Rs 10,000 pe nahi) — toh Rs 12,544. Third year 12% Rs 12,544 pe — Rs 14,049. Dekho, tumne sirf Rs 10,000 lagaye the lekin teen saal mein Rs 4,049 extra bane — bina kuch kiye!

Real Example — Rs 5,000 SIP

Agar tum aaj Rs 5,000 per month SIP start karo 12% expected return ke saath, toh 10 saal mein tumhara Rs 6 lakh invest hua aur value hogi roughly Rs 11.6 lakh. 20 saal mein Rs 12 lakh invest — value Rs 49.5 lakh. 30 saal mein Rs 18 lakh invest — value Rs 1.76 CRORE! Tumne sirf Rs 18 lakh diye aur compounding ne Rs 1.58 crore kamake diye. Ye hai compounding ka jadoo.

Time Sabse Important Hai

Compounding mein sabse important factor TIME hai — kitna zyada time doge, utna zyada powerful hoga. 22 saal ki age pe start karna vs 32 saal pe — sirf 10 saal ka fark crores ka fark bana deta hai. Isliye sabse achhi advice hai — JALDI START KARO. Amount chhoti ho toh bhi chalega, Rs 500 se bhi start karo — lekin start karo.

Kahan Invest Karein Compounding Ke Liye?

Equity mutual funds (12-15% long term return), Nifty 50 Index Fund (market average return), Blue chip stocks direct (dividend reinvest karo), PPF (7.1% guaranteed, 15 saal lock-in). Best approach — equity mutual fund mein SIP 20+ saal ke liye. Boring hai? Haan. Effective hai? BOHOT.

Compounding Ka Dushman — Interruption

Sabse badi galti jo log karte hain — beech mein paisa nikal lete hain. Market gira toh darr ke SIP band kar di, ya koi kharcha aa gaya toh redeem kar liya. Har baar aisa karte ho toh compounding chain toot jaati hai aur phir se zero se start hoti hai. Discipline sabse zaroori hai — set karo aur bhool jao.
