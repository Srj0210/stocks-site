Share Market Kya Hai? Complete Beginner Guide in Hindi

By SRJahir Tech · March 2026 · 10 min read

Share market — kuch log ise lottery samajhte hain, kuch log darr jaate hain, aur kuch log isse crorepati ban jaate hain. Asli baat ye hai ki share market na lottery hai na juaa — ye ek system hai jahan companies apne shares (hisse) bechti hain aur log un shares ko buy-sell karte hain. Chalein ekdum basics se samajhte hain.

Share Kya Hota Hai?

Share matlab kisi company ka chhota sa hissa. Agar Reliance Industries ke total 1000 crore shares hain aur tumne 100 shares kharide, toh tum Reliance ke 0.00000001% owner ho. Chota hissa hai, lekin owner ho. Company ko profit ho toh tumhe bhi milta hai (dividend ke form mein), company ki value badhe toh tumhare shares ki value bhi badhe.

Stock Exchange Kya Hai?

Stock exchange wo jagah hai jahan shares ki buying-selling hoti hai. India mein 2 main exchanges hain — NSE (National Stock Exchange) aur BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange). NSE ka indicator Nifty 50 hai aur BSE ka Sensex. Almost sab trading ab online hoti hai — tum ghar baithe phone se shares buy-sell kar sakte ho.

Share Market Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

Simple process hai — Company ko paisa chahiye growth ke liye, toh wo IPO laati hai aur public ko shares bechti hai. IPO ke baad shares stock exchange pe list ho jaate hain. Ab koi bhi share buy ya sell kar sakta hai. Price demand aur supply se decide hoti hai — zyada log kharidna chahein toh price badhta hai, zyada log bechna chahein toh girata hai.

Share Market Mein Paisa Kaise Banta Hai?

Do tarike hain — Capital Gains (sasta khareed ke mehnga becho) aur Dividends (company apne profit ka kuch hissa shareholders ko deti hai). Long term mein Nifty 50 ne roughly 12-15% annual return diya hai — matlab agar tumne 10 saal pehle Rs 1 lakh invest kiya hota toh aaj Rs 3-4 lakh hote.

Kaise Start Karein?

Step 1: Demat account kholo (Zerodha, Groww pe free hai). Step 2: Pehle blue chip stocks samjho. Step 3: Chhoti amount se start karo — Rs 500 se bhi SIP ho sakta hai. Step 4: Tax rules samjho. Step 5: Patience rakho — share market mein jaldi ameer hone ki soch se log paisa LOSE karte hain.

Share market ki latest updates ke liye humari home page pe live indices, news, aur IPOs check karo!
