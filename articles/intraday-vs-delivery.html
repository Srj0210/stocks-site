Intraday vs Delivery Trading — Kaunsa Better Hai?

By SRJahir Tech · March 2026 · 8 min read

Naye investors ke mann mein ye sawaal zaroor aata hai — stocks same din bechna chahiye ya hold karna chahiye? Ye dono alag approaches hain — Intraday trading aur Delivery trading. Dono ke apne pros aur cons hain. Chalein samjhte hain detail mein.

Delivery Trading Kya Hai?

Delivery trading matlab tum stock buy karo aur apne demat account mein hold karo — 1 din, 1 mahina, 1 saal, 10 saal — jab tak chaho. Shares actually tumhare naam pe transfer hote hain. Ye traditional investing hai — jo Warren Buffett karta hai. Most successful investors delivery investors hain.

Intraday Trading Kya Hai?

Intraday matlab tum stock buy karo aur SAME DIN market close hone se pehle bech do. Tum stock ko own nahi karte — sirf price movement se profit/loss book karte ho. Agar 100 shares Rs 500 pe buy kiye aur Rs 510 pe bech diye same din — Rs 1000 profit. Agar Rs 490 pe bechna padaa — Rs 1000 loss.

Key Differences

Holding period — Delivery mein unlimited, Intraday mein same day mandatory sell. Risk — Delivery mein limited (company band nahi hogi toh kuch toh milega), Intraday mein position size badi hoti hai leverage ki wajah se toh loss bhi bada. Charges — Delivery pe Zerodha mein 0 brokerage, Intraday pe Rs 20 per trade. Tax — Delivery ke long term gains pe 12.5% LTCG (1.25L exempt), Intraday pe income tax slab rate. Capital — Delivery mein full amount chahiye, Intraday mein broker 5x-20x leverage deta hai (Rs 10,000 se Rs 50,000-2,00,000 ke stocks trade kar sakte ho).

Reality Check — Intraday Mein Kitne Log Paisa Kamate Hain?

SEBI ke data ke according, approximately 90% intraday traders paisa LOSE karte hain. Ye koi joke nahi hai — 10 mein se 9 log loss mein rehte hain. Reasons — emotional trading, overconfidence, no stop loss, leverage ka galat use, aur market ko predict karna nearly impossible hai short term mein. Jo 10% profit karte hain unke paas years of experience, strict discipline, aur proper risk management hoti hai.

Meri Recommendation

Beginners ke liye 100% delivery investing recommended hai. Blue chip stocks mein SIP approach se start karo. Kam se kam 1-2 saal market experience le lo. Agar phir bhi intraday try karna hai toh paper trading se start karo (virtual money se practice). Real money se intraday tab karo jab consistently paper trading mein profit ho.
