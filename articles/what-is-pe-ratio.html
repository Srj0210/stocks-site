PE Ratio Kya Hai? Stock Valuation Samjhein Simple Mein

By SRJahir Tech · March 2026 · 7 min read

Stock market mein sabse zyada use hone wala number PE Ratio hai. Jab bhi koi stock mehnga hai ya sasta — PE Ratio dekhke decide karte hain. Lekin exactly ye kya hai aur kaise use karna chahiye? Simple language mein samjhate hain.

PE Ratio Kya Hai?

PE ka full form hai Price to Earnings Ratio. Ye batata hai ki tum company ke har 1 rupee ke profit ke liye kitna pay kar rahe ho. Formula simple hai — Stock Price divide by Earnings Per Share (EPS). Agar stock ka price Rs 500 hai aur EPS Rs 25 hai, toh PE = 500/25 = 20. Matlab tum har Rs 1 profit ke liye Rs 20 pay kar rahe ho.

High PE vs Low PE

High PE (30+) matlab market ko lagta hai company future mein bohot grow karegi — isliye log zyada pay karne ko taiyar hain. Example: IT companies often PE 25-35 pe trade karti hain. Low PE (10-15) matlab ya toh company slow growth hai ya market ne undervalue kar diya hai. Banks often PE 10-18 pe trade karti hain. Very High PE (50+) matlab either company bohot fast grow kar rahi hai (like Zomato) ya overvalued hai — risky zone.

PE Se Stock Compare Kaise Karein?

Important rule — PE ratio sirf same sector ki companies mein compare karo. TCS (IT) ka PE 28 hai aur SBI (Bank) ka PE 12 hai — iska matlab SBI sasta hai aur TCS mehnga hai ye galat comparison hai. IT sector ka average PE 25+ hota hai, banking ka 12-15. Compare karo TCS vs Infosys, ya SBI vs HDFC Bank — tab PE useful hai.

PE Ke Limitations

PE ratio perfect nahi hai. Loss-making companies ka PE nahi hota (negative earnings). Cyclical companies (metals, sugar) mein PE misleading hota hai — low PE pe buy karna aur high PE pe sell karna galat ho sakta hai inme. One-time profit ya loss PE ko temporarily distort kar sakta hai. Isliye PE ko akela mat dekho — doosre ratios bhi check karo jaise PB Ratio, ROE, Debt to Equity.

Nifty 50 Ka PE Ratio

Nifty 50 index ka bhi ek overall PE hota hai. Historically Nifty PE 18-22 ke range mein rehta hai. 22+ ho toh market overvalued hai — cautious raho. 15 se neeche ho toh undervalued — buying opportunity. 2020 COVID crash mein Nifty PE 17 tak gir gaya tha — jinke paas himmat thi unhone bohot paisa banaya wahan invest karke.
