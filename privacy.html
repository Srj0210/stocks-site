1. Information We Collect

stocks.srjahir.in is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily contact us.

We may automatically collect certain non-personal information through third-party services, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This data is collected through Google Analytics and Google AdSense.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. This helps us improve our site and user experience.

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

4. Cookies

This site uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect some functionality of the website.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates the following third-party services:

Google Analytics (traffic analysis), Google AdSense (advertisements), TradingView (stock charts), and Google Apps Script (data backend via Google Sheets). Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data collection practices.

6. Data Storage

If you use the Portfolio Tracker feature, your stock holdings data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.
